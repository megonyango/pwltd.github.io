What we do?

PRINCEWORK MEDICAL

Princework Medical focuses on hospital design and fit out. We have worked with several clients in the field to come up with well functional hospital spaces which look great. Our designers are well trained and expirienced to give solutions to some of the challenges we have in hospital spaces.

PRINCEWORK HOMES

Our focus in homes is design and build of kitchen spaces ,cabinets,storage and joinery based home furniture. we do design of the spaces and recommend types of interiors and materials to be used ranging from steel kitchens to boards depending on the clients preference

PRINCEWORK PRODUCTS

Princework products includes a range of products for hospitals,homes and offices. some of the products include : workstations ,examination couches,consultation desks storage and accessories.For homes we do wardrobes,TV stands, dressing tables, beds and many more home based furniture
